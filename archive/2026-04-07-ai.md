### 📰 1. Bezos 挖角 xAI 联合创始人 / Bezos' Project Prometheus Poaches xAI Co-founder from OpenAI

**发生了什么 (What happened):**
Jeff Bezos 的 AI 实验室（Project Prometheus）成功挖走了 xAI 联合创始人 Kyle Kozic（他此前在 OpenAI 任职）。Kozic 将在 Bezos 领导的初创公司专注于 AI 基础设施建设，该公司正致力于利用 AI 改进制造业，已获超 60 亿美元融资。这是近期 AI 领域人才争夺战的最新动向。

*Jeff Bezos' AI lab (Project Prometheus) has poached xAI co-founder Kyle Kozic, who had been working at OpenAI. Kozic will focus on AI infrastructure at the well-funded startup, which Bezos co-founded alongside former Google exec Vik Bajaj. Project Prometheus focuses on using AI to improve manufacturing — and has already raised $6.2B. The latest move in the ongoing AI talent war.*

**为什么重要 (Why it matters):**
- 顶尖 AI 人才的流动正在加速，科技巨头和资金雄厚的初创公司都在争夺核心基础设施专家。
- AI 在制造业（实体经济）的应用正成为下一个重要战场。

🔗 **来源 (Source):** [The Verge - Jeff Bezos' AI lab poaches xAI cofounder Kyle Kozic from OpenAI](https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence)

### 📰 2. Anthropic 与谷歌和博通达成重大基础设施协议 / Anthropic Signs Major Infrastructure Deal with Google and Broadcom

**发生了什么 (What happened):**
Anthropic 宣布与谷歌和博通（Broadcom）签署了一项大型 AI 基础设施协议。预计从 2027 年开始，将有“数千兆瓦（multiple gigawatts）的下一代 TPU 算力”上线，用于“驱动我们的前沿 Claude 模型”。此外，该公司表示其年化收入已超过 300 亿美元。

*Anthropic has signed a massive AI infrastructure deal with Google and Broadcom. "Multiple gigawatts of next-generation TPU capacity" are expected to come online starting in 2027 to "power our frontier Claude models." The company also reported its run-rate revenue has surpassed $30 billion.*

**为什么重要 (Why it matters):**
- **算力即权力：** 确保未来的计算资源是训练下一代大模型的关键。
- **TPU 生态：** Anthropic 选择谷歌的 TPU 而非完全依赖 Nvidia GPU，显示了 AI 硬件市场的多元化趋势。
- **惊人的商业化速度：** 300 亿美元的年化收入表明企业级 AI 市场的需求极其强劲。

🔗 **来源 (Source):** [The Verge - Anthropic has signed a big AI infrastructure deal with Google and Broadcom](https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence)

### 📰 3. 谷歌推出免费离线 AI 听写应用 / Google Launches Free Offline AI Dictation App

**发生了什么 (What happened):**
谷歌推出了一款名为 "Google AI Edge Eloquent" 的全新实时 AI 转录应用。该应用免费、无需订阅、没有使用限制，并且可以在离线状态下自动润色你的语音（例如过滤掉“嗯”、“啊”等语气词）。目前仅支持 iOS，但计划未来登陆 Android 和 macOS。

*Google has launched "Google AI Edge Eloquent," a new live AI transcription app that is free, offline, requires no subscription, and has no usage limits. It automatically polishes your speech by filtering out filler words like "um." Currently on iOS, with plans for Android and macOS.*

**为什么重要 (Why it matters):**
- **端侧 AI (Edge AI) 的胜利：** 强大的语音处理能力现在可以直接在手机本地运行，无需将音频发送到云端，极大地保护了隐私并降低了延迟。
- **实用工具的普及：** AI 正在从“聊天机器人”变成无缝融入日常工作流的隐形助手。

🔗 **来源 (Source):** [The Verge - Google has launched a free, offline AI dictation app](https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence)
